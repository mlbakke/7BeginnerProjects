Dr. Martin Luther King

The Civil Rights Movement Leader with a dream

Timeline of Martin Luther King's life

  • 1929 - Martin Luther King, Jr. is born
  • 1948 - King graduates from Morehouse College with a Bachelor's Degree in sociology.
  • 1951 - King graduates with a Bachelor's Degree in Divinity studies.
  • 1953 - King marries Coretta Scott in Marion, Alabama.
  • 1955 - King receives his PhD from Boston University.
  • 1957 - Southern Christian Leadership Conference forms in Atlanta, electing King president. King is featured on the cover of Time Magazine.
  • 1959 - King studies non-violent tactics during a trip to India.
  • 1963 - 250,000 people march on Washington, and King delivers his "I Have a Dream" speech.
  • 1964 - Time Magazine names King 'Man of the Year'. King Receives the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1967 - King announces his vision of a Poor People's March on Washington.
  • 1968 - King is assassinated on the balcony of the Lorraine Motel, in Memphis.
